Smart Mobility

Among twenty-two smart city programs, smart mobility plan stands out. Barcelona's 2013/2018 Urban mobility plan(UMP) aim to build a more sustainable, efficient, equitable, safer and healthier collective mobility role model. Which echo with the growth of social and economic progress aw well as quality of life in a city.
As the comparison shows below: the air quality improvement in six years(left), and the bike path extension in four years(right).

superblock

nine grid

The Superblock is a model of mobility that are made up of a grid of basic roads forming a polygon. It restructures the typical urban road network, provides solutions to the main problems of urban mobility.
The interior is closed to motorized vehicles and above ground parking, and gives preference to pedestrian traffic in the public space. Though the inner streets are generally reserved for pedestrians, they can be used by residential traffic, services, emergency vehicles, and loading/unloading vehicles under special circumstances.

how it works

With Superblock model, the urban quality improves in many levels evidently. Space for pedestrians increased 50%, accessibility increased 10% and reached a total of 99%, and livability index in public space improved nearly 50%.
From 2013-2018, with PMU actions the situation turned to a desired mobility model. The walking and bicycling ratio increased obviously with the decrease of private vehicles.

Urban Mobility Plan(PMU)

Barcelona's 2013/2018 Urban Mobility Plan (PMU) is a planning tool defining the lines of action that will govern urban mobility in the coming years. Here list the main lines of action

SUPERBLOCKS

ORGANIZATION OF THE CITY’S URBAN PATTERN IN SUPERBLOCKS AND OTHER MEASURES

BUS

IMPLEMENTATION OF THE NEW ORTHOGONAL BUS NETWORK

CYCLING

TOTAL DEVELOPMENT OF CYCLING NETWORK

TRAFFIC

MANTAIN THE CURRENT LEVEL OF TRAFFIC SERVICE

ENVIRONMENT

COMPLIANCE WITH REGULATORY PARAMETERS OF ENVIRONMENTAL QUALITY

OCCUPANCY VEHICLES

PROMOTION AND POSITIVE DISCRIMINATION MEASURES OF HIGH OCCUPANCY VEHICLES

PARKING

REVIEW OF THE REGULATION OF PARKING ON AND OFF ROAD

LOADING AND UNLOADING

IMPROVING THE EFFICIENCY OF LOADING AND UNLOADING

City of People


According to the Sustainable Cities Index 2016, Barcelona is one of the top 25 global cities in terms of environmental sustainability. And according to the Eurobarometer Quality of Life in European Cities 2015 report, that published by the European Commission in 2016, 92% of Barcelona’s residents are satisfied with the city and the neighborhood where they live, which is one of the highest percentages recorded amongst the 79 cities analyzes. Aspects of the city like public space, security, public transport, or confidence in other residents got very good ratings from residents regarding their own city.
